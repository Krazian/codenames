How to Use
  1. Spymasters can use the key card OR the official Codename Gadget app to determine:
    • Who goes first.
    • Where the red, blue, civilian, and assassin cards are.
  2. Click "New Game" to reset the board.
  3. When players choose a card/word, spymasters click the word and choose the corresponding color that appears on the key.
  4. If teams are taking too long to make a guess, click "Start Timer" to give them a 30 second countdown.
  5. Click "Reset Timer" to cancel a countdown and set the timer back to 30 seconds.
  6. Have fun!
